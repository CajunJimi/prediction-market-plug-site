How this works: For each resolved trade, we simulate placing $100 on the same outcome. Win profit = ($100 / entry_price) − $100 Loss profit = −$100 A wallet that bought YES at 50¢ and won pays out $200 → +$100 profit. One that bought YES at 90¢ and won pays out $111 → +$11 profit. This reflects the actual odds-adjusted return, not just raw win rate. Entry price matters.
