Copy-Trade Strategy
  • Best targets: Bots with >70% win rate, >1,000 resolved trades, and positive P&L. High trade count = statistically significant edge.
  • How to copy: Click any wallet to open their full profile. Monitor their open positions and follow their entries on markets where they have a proven edge.
  • Caution: Some bots are market-makers (high volume, ~50% WR, near-zero P&L) — they profit from the spread, not directional bets. These aren't useful copy targets.
  • Bot score breakdown: Hover over any bot score to see why it was flagged — same-second fills, burst patterns, model-driven staking, and concentrated hours.
How Bot Detection Works
  • Same-second fills (30%): Humans can't execute multiple trades in the same second. This is the strongest bot signal.
  • Trade frequency (25%): Median gap <30s between trades = API-driven execution.
  • Burst patterns (20%): Bots trade in rapid bursts separated by long pauses — a bimodal timing distribution.
  • Model-driven staking (10%): Sophisticated bots use Kelly criterion — highly variable position sizes (unlike naive uniform-size scripts).
  • Hour concentration (15%): Winning bots use 3–5 hour windows. Market-maker bots run 24/7. Humans trade across 8–14 waking hours.
