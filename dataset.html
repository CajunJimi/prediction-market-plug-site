Why This Matters

Most prediction market tools scrape data on-demand. We instrument every event at ingestion time — price moves, volume surges, whale entries, odds shifts — and attach a ground-truth outcome label 24–72h later when the market resolves.

The result: a fully-labelled, timestamped event dataset that grows automatically. No human labelling cost. No API rate limits. Just a database that compounds.
