FAQ

Common questions

What is a prediction market?

Prediction markets let you trade on the outcome of real-world events — politics, crypto prices, sports, and more. Platforms like Polymarket and Kalshi let you buy "YES" or "NO" shares that pay $1 if the event resolves in your favour. Prices reflect the crowd's probability estimate in real time.

How do the whale alerts work?

Our bot polls the Polymarket trade API every 10 seconds and cross-references large trades ($5K+) against the leaderboard to identify whether the trader is a known high-performer. Alerts include their win rate, leaderboard rank, and trade details — giving you context, not just a number.

What are order book signals?

We connect directly to Polymarket's CLOB (Central Limit Order Book) WebSocket and track real-time order depth. When spreads widen, liquidity collapses, or a large sweep happens, we alert you — typically 30–120 seconds before a price-based detector would fire. These are the closest thing to "order flow" on Polymarket.
