The Story
  • 💬

    Discord as a Growth Engine

    A paying Discord community (Free / Pro / Gold tiers) provides capital and user feedback. Revenue funds infrastructure and validates product-market fit while the dataset is being built.

  • 📦

    Proprietary Dataset as the Moat

    The real asset is not the trading bot — it's the enriched, labelled prediction-market dataset: 3.75M+ signal events, 796 wallet behavioural profiles, 97K+ markets, all timestamped and outcome-labelled.

  • 🧠

    Self-Improving Model

    Every resolved market is a ground-truth label. The signal scoring model retrains continuously. Calibration curves improve every week — this is a compounding data flywheel.

  • 🚀

    Expansion to Institutional Intelligence

    The long-term play: license the dataset, the API, and the trained models to hedge funds, market-makers, and prediction-market protocol teams who need calibrated event-outcome intelligence.
