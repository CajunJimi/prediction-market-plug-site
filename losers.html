💡 How to Use This for Marketing Opportunities
  • Counter-trade signals: Wallets consistently below 40% win rate are losing on both sides of markets — when they make a large bet, the contrarian position has positive expected value.
  • Content & outreach: High-volume losers are actively engaged bettors who clearly want to win. They're the most likely to pay for better tools, signals, or coaching.
  • Pattern analysis: Click any wallet to open their full profile and see which categories they keep losing in — they may be experts in one area and terrible in others.
  • Confidence vs accuracy: Sort by "Worst Win Rate" and look for wallets with high average trade sizes — they're spending big with conviction and losing. Prime target for copy-trade counter-positioning.
⚠️ Important: Polymarket's own leaderboard uses unrealized mark-to-market PNL on open positions — a wallet can show as a "winner" on Polymarket while having deeply negative resolved P&L. Our win rate is based on resolved trades only (actual outcomes), which is a more reliable signal. Click any wallet name to see their full equity curve on the Wallet page.
