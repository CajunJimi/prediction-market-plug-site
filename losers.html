How to Use This for Marketing Opportunities
  • Counter-trade signals: Wallets consistently below 40% win rate are losing on both sides of markets — when they make a large bet, the contrarian position has positive expected value.
  • Content & outreach: High-volume losers are actively engaged bettors who clearly want to win. They're the most likely to pay for better tools, signals, or coaching.
  • Pattern analysis: Click any wallet to open their full profile and see which categories they keep losing in.
