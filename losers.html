How to Use This Data
  • Counter-trade signals: Wallets with <40% win rate and high volume are consistently wrong with conviction. When they bet big, the other side has positive expected value.
  • Copy-trade bots: Switch to "Winning Bots" tab to find algorithmic traders with high win rates — these are your copy-trade targets.
  • Marketing targets: High-volume losers are actively engaged bettors who want to win. They're the most likely to pay for better signals and tools.
  • Bot detection: Hover over any bot score to see the breakdown — frequency, timing regularity, size uniformity, 24/7 activity, and execution speed.
About the data: "Real P&L" is computed from every resolved trade on the Goldsky subgraph — not the misleading mark-to-market figure on Polymarket's leaderboard. Bot scores are computed from trade pattern analysis (timing, sizing, hours of activity). Click any wallet to see their full equity curve on the Wallet page. Run the crawler (npm run crawl-wallets) to expand coverage.
